Resume

A problem solver of multiple skillsets

My career has been marked by finding solutions to problems and making sure all the pieces of the puzzle fit neatly together in the end. I'm a creative and resourceful technician who always searches for the most efficient and graceful solution to a problem, and only uses a sledge hammer when absolutely necessary. (I once ran a billing system for a small telephone company in NYC using only WordPerfect and Lotus Notes.) I'm largely self-taught in most of my skills, the result of realizing that problems only get solved if someone takes responsibilty. I may never win a hackathon but I also won't avoid work to play golf. I've learned a little bit about a lot of things and a lot about a few things, but in that regard repetition and Stackoverflow are my closest friends.

It's an exciting time to be in our industry and I've been around long enough to tell a few stories. We've finally reached a point where it's cost effective to create a large solution for a small organization as well as a large solution for a large corporation using the same toolsets. A flower stand and an airline can both use the same technology and it scales nicely in both cases. I stepped out of the center lane for a few years and have found that things are changing by larger degrees every year, but my career has been a steady diet of starting fires with wet matches, and so I'm prepared to be nimble and use lessons learned to take on the challenge of an ever-changing digital world.
